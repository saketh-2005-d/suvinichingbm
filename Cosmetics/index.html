Complete Beauty Guides

Men's Makeup Routine (6-Step)

  1. Prep: Cleanse with Cetaphil and apply moisturizer
  2. Concealer: Use Maybelline Fit Me on blemishes
  3. Powder: Set with Laura Mercier Translucent Powder
  4. Finishing: Apply Burt's Bees Tinted Lip Balm
  5. Touch-ups: Carry powder for midday application
  6. Setting: Use MAC Fix+ for longevity

Women's Makeup Routine (7-Step)

  1. Prep: Cleanse and apply skincare routine
  2. Primer: Apply makeup primer for base
  3. Base: Apply foundation and concealer
  4. Eyes: Urban Decay Naked3, eyeliner, mascara
  5. Cheeks: Tarte Blush and Becca Highlighter
  6. Lips: MAC Lipstick or Tinted Lip Balm
  7. Set: Use setting spray for all-day wear

Daily Skincare (10-Step K-Beauty)

  1. Oil Cleanser: Remove makeup and oil
  2. Water-Based Cleanser: Cetaphil gentle wash
  3. Toner: Thayers Rose Water Toner
  4. Essence: Lightweight hydrating essence
  5. Serum: The Ordinary Hyaluronic Acid
  6. Sunscreen: Morning protection (Neutrogena)
  7. Moisturizer: CeraVe Facial Lotion
  8. Eye Cream: Kiehl's Avocado Eye Care
  9. Weekly: Aztec Clay Mask 1-2 times
  10. Exfoliate: Paula's Choice 1-2 times weekly

Product Selection Tips

  • Foundation Match: Test on jawline in natural light
  • Patch Test: Test for allergies before full use
  • Skin Tone: Match depth and undertone correctly
  • Ingredients: Check labels for potential irritants
  • Hypoallergenic: Choose if sensitive skin
  • Quality Brands: MAC, Urban Decay, Tarte, CeraVe
  • Read Reviews: Check trusted beauty sites
  • Expiry Date: Note shelf life of products

Know Your Skin Type

Oily Skin

Characteristics: Shiny, prone to acne, enlarged pores

Best Products: Oil-free moisturizers, BHA exfoliants (Paula's Choice), mattifying powders (Laura Mercier)

Dry Skin

Characteristics: Flaky, tight, rough texture

Best Products: Rich moisturizers (CeraVe), hydrating serums (The Ordinary HA), creamy cleansers (Cetaphil)

Combination Skin

Characteristics: Oily T-zone, dry cheeks, mixed concerns

Best Products: Balanced formulas, targeted zone treatments, lightweight moisturizers (CeraVe)

Sensitive Skin

Characteristics: Easily irritated, reactive, red patches

Best Products: Hypoallergenic (Cetaphil), fragrance-free (Thayers), minimal ingredients, soothing formulas
