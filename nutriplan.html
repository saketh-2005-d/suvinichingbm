ℹ️ About NutriPlan

Our Mission

NutriPlan is dedicated to helping individuals achieve their health and wellness goals through personalized nutrition planning and expert guidance.

Key Features

  • Accurate BMI calculation and health assessment
  • Personalized meal plans based on dietary preferences
  • Comprehensive recipe database with nutritional info
  • Allergy and dietary restriction management
  • Daily calorie tracking and recommendations
  • Evidence-based nutrition advice
  • AI-powered personalized nutrition advisor

Health Tips

  • Drink at least 2-3 liters of water daily
  • Include fruits and vegetables in every meal
  • Exercise for at least 30 minutes daily
  • Get 7-9 hours of quality sleep
  • Reduce processed foods and added sugars
  • Maintain consistent meal times
